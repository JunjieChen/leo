In this package, there are many folders. Next I explain each folder in detail.

figure: the figures in our paper, which are more clear and larger
tool: the source code of our tool, including method optimization code, library optimization code and optimization tuning code---program-splitter and local-optimizer
results: all the experiemntal results in our study
script: the scripts used in our study
mutants: all the mutants used in our study
subject: the GNU Coreutils programs used in our study
